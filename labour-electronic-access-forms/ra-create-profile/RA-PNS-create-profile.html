Labour Electronic Access Forms (LEAF) Online Service - Privacy Notice to Applicants

You may want to print this page for future reference, since it contains important information.

Protecting your personal information and privacy is important to us.

All personal information provided as part of the registration and use of the Labour Electronic Access Forms (LEAF) Online Service is collected under the authority of the Department of Employment and Social Development Act and the Canada Labour Code for the purpose of administering the Labour programs available through this system and for the purposes of assessing reports submitted by your organization to the Labour Program. Completion of the LEAF Online Service registration process is voluntary; however, refusal to provide the required information will result in not obtaining access to LEAF Online Service.

As part of the assessment process, the information may be shared with external consultants, review committee members, officials in other federal departments, provincial and/or territorial governments or Members of Parliament, based on the requirements of the program.

The information you provide may also be used and/or disclosed for policy analysis, research and evaluation purposes.

In the event that the information provided contains personal information, this information will be administered in accordance with the Department of Employment and Social Development Act, the Privacy Act and other applicable laws. Under the Privacy Act, you have the right to the protection of, and access to your personal information, which is described in Personal Information Bank ESDC PPU 298. Instructions for making formal requests about your personal information are outlined in the government publication entitled Info Source, which is available at the following web site address: www.infosource.gc.ca. Info Source may also be accessed on-line at any Service Canada Centre.

The LEAF Online Service is an initiative of Employment and Social Development Canada (ESDC). It is an Internet application that is owned and administered by ESDC. The purpose of the LEAF Online Service is to help organizations meet their Occupational Health and Safety reporting obligations under the Canada Labour Code.

These Terms of Use contain many provisions that may affect your rights and obligations. These will be automatically rescinded if your organization is not successfully authenticated and as such, a LEAF Online Service account will not be created.

The activation of your account within the LEAF Online Service, and future access and use of the service are subject to (1) your acceptance of, and your compliance with these Terms, and (2) the authentication of your organization. If you are the Primary Officer (see definition below) of your organization, your individual identity will also need to be authenticated and authorization will be required from the head of your organization to complete the registration process.
