Grants and Contributions Online Services (GCOS) - Notice to Applicants

You may want to print this page for future reference, since it contains important information.

Protecting your personal information and privacy is important to us.

The information you provide when creating your Grants and Contributions (Gs&Cs) Online Services (GCOS) account and when using GCOS is collected under the authority of the Department of Employment and Social Development Act for the purpose of administering Gs&Cs programs available through this system and for the purposes of assessing the merits of any Gs&Cs application submitted by your organization to the Department. Completion of the GCOS registration process is voluntary; however, refusal to provide the required information will result in not obtaining access to GCOS.

As part of the assessment process, the information may be shared with external consultants, review committee members, officials in other federal departments, provincial and/or territorial governments or Members of Parliament, based on the requirements of the program.

The information you provide may also be used and/or disclosed for policy analysis, research and evaluation purposes. However, these additional uses and/or disclosures of your personal information will never result in an administrative decision being made about you.

In the event that the information provided contains personal information, this information will be administered in accordance with the Department of Employment and Social Development Act, the Privacy Act and other applicable laws. Under the Privacy Act, you have the right to the protection of, and access to your personal information, which is described in Personal Information Bank ESDC PPU 298. Instructions for making formal requests about your personal information are outlined in the government publication entitled Info Source, which is available at the following web site address: www.infosource.gc.ca.. Info Source may also be accessed on-line at any Service Canada Centre.

TERMS OF USE

The Grants and Contributions Online Services (GCOS) is an initiative of Employment and Social Development Canada (ESDC). It is an Internet application that is owned and administered by ESDC.
The purpose of the GCOS is to provide organizations the ability to

  1. Establish a GCOS account and manage user and organization profiles;
  2. Electronically submit applications for funding and other supporting documents; and
  3. Electronically submit documents that may be required by the terms and conditions of an approved funding agreement.

These Terms of Use contain many provisions that may affect your rights and obligations. These Terms will be automatically rescinded if your organization is not successfully authenticated and as such, a GCOS organization account will not be created.

The activation of your account within the GCOS, and future access and use of the GCOS are subject to (1) your acceptance of, and your compliance with these Terms, and (2) the authentication of your organization. If you are the Primary Officer (see definition below) of your organization, your individual identity will also need to be authenticated and authorization will be required from the head of your organization to complete the registration process.
